campus life

school auditorium

was established in 2002

The School Auditorium is a big auditorium with many orange seats on the backside and an area where students can play badminton. There are six doors for entering and exiting the auditorium. In the auditorium assemblies are also held.

school library

was established in 2000

The School Library is a library with a very good collection of books, there children can sit comfortably. Sometimes we can issue books and after a specific time we have to return them.

school computer room

was established in 2011

The School Computer room is a room with lots and lots of computers. It is full of gaming headphones and the computers are of the newest version.
